Idea hurdle: don't underline. The Mozilla Foundation has a good article about the changes to The HTML Unarticulated Annotation Element. So before you ask, read the article. We'll get to an acceptable way to underline when we get to CSS.

With that out of the way, here's the lesson:

< sup >

< sub >
